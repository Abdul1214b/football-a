Lionel Messi holds the record with 8 Ballon d'Or awards, followed by Cristiano Ronaldo with 5. This dominance spans over a decade of football excellence.

Consecutive Wins

Lionel Messi won 4 consecutive Ballon d'Or awards from 2009 to 2012, a record achievement that may never be broken in modern football.

Global Recognition

The Ballon d'Or is considered the most prestigious individual award in football worldwide, recognizing the best player of each calendar year.
